Geoffrey Hinton

The Godfather of Deep Learning

Discover the work of the computer scientist whose research sparked the modern AI revolution.

The Backpropagation Breakthrough

In the 1980s, Geoffrey Hinton and his colleagues made a major discovery: **backpropagation**.

This algorithm, though theorized earlier, became practical and allowed complex neural networks to "learn" by adjusting their internal connections based on their errors.

The Deep Learning Revolution

AlexNet and ImageNet

In 2012, Hinton and his students created **AlexNet**, a deep neural network that won the ImageNet computer vision competition by a huge margin.

A Tipping Point

This event showed the world the true power of deep learning and kicked off the current AI boom, leading to breakthroughs in everything from self-driving cars to voice assistants.

A Lasting Legacy

Geoffrey Hinton's work laid the groundwork for the modern AI that we see all around us. He's a recipient of the Turing Award, considered the "Nobel Prize of computing," and his insights continue to shape the direction of research and development in the field.
