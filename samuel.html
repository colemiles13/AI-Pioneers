Arthur Samuel

The Pioneer of Machine Learning

Learn about the man who taught a computer to learn on its own and gave us the field of machine learning.

The Checkers-Playing Computer

In the 1950s, Arthur Samuel created a computer program that played the game of **checkers**.

The program was able to learn from its own mistakes. It would get better at the game the more it played, eventually beating its own creator!

What Is Machine Learning?

Defining the Field

Arthur Samuel famously defined **machine learning** as: "The field of study that gives computers the ability to learn without being explicitly programmed."

Beyond Checkers

His simple checkers program showed the world that computers could be taught to improve their own performance and find new solutions without human guidance.
